Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know!

What colors do the watches come in?

Watches come in red, orange, green, blue, purple, pink, black, and white.

Do the watches come in different sizes?

Yes, they come in small, medium, and large. Our bands are made with a material that mold to fit the shape of your wrist for a comfortable fit

How soon will I get my new watch?

Our product typically ships in 3-5 business days. We also offer rushed shipping and overnight delivery for an additional fee.

Can VibrWatches ship internationally?

Yes! We ship anywhere in the world. International shipping fees apply

How long can I expect my watch to last?

We guarantee our watches to last 20 years. And when VibrWatches are ready to be retired, most of the parts are recyclable or biodegradable.

Are VibrWatches sold in stores?

Right now our watches are only sold online but you might see our watches sold in Target stores starting in 2016.

Are the watches compatible with different phones?

Our watch can sync with the iPhone and Android.

What is your return policy?

We stand behind our products. If you are not satisfied with your VibrWatch, you can return it for a replacement or refund within two years of purchase. VibrWatch’s guarantee doesn’t cover ordinary wear and tear or damage caused by improper use or accidents. If your item has a manufacturing defect in its materials or workmanship, you can return it at any time.
