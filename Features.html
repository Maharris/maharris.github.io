Features

The Perfect Fit

VibrWatches are made out of a unique material that can mold to fit each user's wrist.

Take a Dip

Our watches are water-proof so you can wear your watch in the shower or while you swim.

Touch Screen

Tired of messing with buttons to set an alarm or change the time? Our watches come with HD touch screen making them extremely user-friendly.

Quiet Notifications

VibrWatches' default function is vibrating so you never have to mess with an embarrassing beeping watch again.

Built to Last

In addition to being water-proof, our watches are also shatter-proof and scratch-proof, so you don't have to worry about dropping them. They are ready for any adventure.

Set Reminders

Sync your Google calendar or similar apps and your watch will remind you of your appointments.

Advanced Settings

Besides setting alarms, our watches also come with a stop watch and timer function.

Stay Informed

Sync apps from your phone like calendars, weather, news updates, and fitness apps.

Gentle to the Earth

We use recycled materials in our watches so you can feel good about your purchase.
